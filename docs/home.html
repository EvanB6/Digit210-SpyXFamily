What is Spy X Family About?

Spy x Family is about a spy, Loid Forger, who creates a fake family for a mission, but his new family has secrets too. His wife, Yor Forger, is secretly an assassin, and his daughter, Anya Forger, can read minds. None of them know each other’s true identities, so they all pretend to be normal while living together, leading to a mix of action, comedy, and cute moments.
