Season 1 Overview

Season 2 of Spy × Family keeps the Forger family juggling daggers and dinner plans as their double lives grow even more tangled. Loid presses forward with Operation Strix, Yor steps into the spotlight during a high-stakes luxury cruise mission guarding a targeted family, and Anya continues her chaotic quest to earn school stars. Between espionage, assassination, and elementary school drama, their “ordinary” family life becomes an acrobatic routine of secrets, danger, and unexpected warmth.
